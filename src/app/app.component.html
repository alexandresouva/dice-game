Goal:

Be the first player to reach 100 points.

Rules:

  • Each player takes turns rolling the dice until they choose to stop.
  • On each turn, the player's score is increased by the value of the dice roll.
  • If a player rolls a 1, they lose all points accumulated in that turn, and the turn passes to the next player.
  • When a player chooses to stop, their current score is added to their total score.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins!

Have fun!
